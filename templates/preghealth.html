Nutrition and Exercise During Pregnancy

When it comes to your diet, plan balanced meals. They should contain protein, carbohydrates, fats, vitamins, and minerals. This is no time for fad or low-calorie dieting. In fact, as a general rule, you need to consume about 350-450 more calories per day your second and third trimesters than before you became pregnant..
